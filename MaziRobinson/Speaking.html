Mazi would love to join you for your next event.

Mazi crafts her presentations around the specific needs and goals of the group,allowing audience members to walk away feeling encouraged and empowered

Whether speaking to women’s organizations, churches, parenting groups, educators,or business leaders, it is Mazi’s passion to give voice to the real issues and concerns facing individuals, families, and leaders today.

Mazi is an experienced speaker specializing in personal and professional growth and enrichment.

She engages and inspires her audiences as she empowers and equips them to meet life’s challenges with confidence and courage. She conducts lively and interactive presentations inspiring and motivating individuals to fully seize their personal and professional potential.

As a Licensed Counselor, Mazi brings a unique blend of knowledge, expertise, and real life application to her speaking.

Each speaking session is well researched and thoughtfully prepared as Mazi provides insight into how and why we think, feel, and act the way we do in our individual and relational lives.

Whether focusing on personal or professional related issues, she speaks to the heart of her audience

by addressing such topics as self worth, living courageously, vulnerability, emotionally healthy relationships, worry and anxiety, life stage transitions, effective communication, leadership development, and spirituality.
