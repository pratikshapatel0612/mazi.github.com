Love Can Be Complicated

You were created to love and be loved. But maybe believing you are loved and living out of that identity feels complicated.

Perhaps you’ve been hurt, wounded, and rejected, or you struggle to navigate a challenging relationship with a difficult person in your life.

If you’ve been telling yourself an old, inaccurate story about who you are and your true value, you may be stuck on a treadmill of performance, trying to control and earn love.

Or you may have decided that love is too messy, so you keep others at a distance, never letting yourself be truly known or seen.
