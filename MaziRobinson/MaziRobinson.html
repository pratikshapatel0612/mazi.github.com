Cultivate Counseling

Mazi is the Founder and Director of Cultivate, an Atlanta-based counseling center and community for women. The mission of the practice is to provide women with excellent, affordable counseling services and resources.
Attend a Cultivate Gatherings to connect with women across Atlanta and beyond who are pursuing personal growth and spiritual development.We invite you to join these regular opportunities for connection,as we learn from others’ stories of courage and faith and dig deep into topics that touch our lives.

Rooted & Established Podcast

Mazi hosts Cultivate’s Rooted & Established podcast, created to continue the mental, emotional, and spiritual health conversations that begin in her counseling sessions and speaking events. Each episode is designed to help you continue your healing and transformation journey.
